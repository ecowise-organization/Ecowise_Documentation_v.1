# Government and reporting

> Government surfaces — issue publication, government profile, sustainability report.

## Overview

Government accounts have two primary jobs on Ecowise. First, they publish sustainability
issues — problem statements that organisations can then propose solutions to (covered in
[issues-and-solutions.md](issues-and-solutions.md)). Second, they generate sustainability
reports: a one-click ISO 26000 / GRI-aligned digest of the activity tied to their account
that can be handed off to stakeholders.

The module also owns the government profile (jurisdiction, departments, contact metadata).

## What it does

- Edit government profile.
- Publish, edit, and unpublish sustainability issues.
- Accept a winning solution on a published issue.
- Generate a sustainability report covering the account's footprint.

## Who uses it

Government account holders. Administrators have read access for moderation.

## Key features

- Single-click report generation; no parameter forms.
- Acceptance of a solution unlocks a "promote to project" path for the winning organisation.
