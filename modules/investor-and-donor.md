# Investor and donor

> Pledging support to projects, with an inbox on the receiving side.

## Overview

This module covers the funding side of Ecowise. Any authenticated user can opt into the
Investor role with one tap; doing so unlocks the pledge composer and the investor dashboard.
Investors browse published projects, place pledges (cash or in-kind), and track the status of
their pledges. Project owners receive pledges in an inbox where they can accept or decline,
and they can see at a glance how much their project has attracted in pledge interest.

Pledges are non-binding offers — acceptance is what makes them count.

## What it does

- Enable / disable the Investor role on the user's own account.
- Compose a pledge against a project (amount, kind, message).
- Project owner inbox: accept, decline, or leave pending.
- Pledger can withdraw a pending pledge.

## Who uses it

Anyone with the Investor role grant on the pledger side; organisation owners on the receiving
side.

## Key features

- Investor role is reversible without losing pledge history.
- Pledge inbox is the bridge between donor intent and the project owner's roadmap.
