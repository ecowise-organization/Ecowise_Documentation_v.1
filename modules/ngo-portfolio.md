# NGO portfolio

> The organisation's at-a-glance view of its work on the platform.

## Overview

The NGO portfolio is a read-side rollup that an organisation account uses to see itself: the
projects it owns, the solutions it has had accepted ("Wins"), its public organisation profile,
and its verification state. It is not a feature in its own right so much as a presentation
layer over data owned by other modules — but for an NGO it is the most-visited surface after
the dashboard.

## What it does

- Lists owned projects with health and funding indicators.
- Surfaces accepted solutions ("Wins") that came in through the issues marketplace.
- Shows the org profile as it appears to the public.
- Indicates verification status (verified, pending review, rejected).

## Who uses it

Organisation account holders.

## Key features

- One stop to monitor the org's footprint without bouncing between issues, projects, and
  profile pages.
- Verification banner with next-step guidance when status is pending.
